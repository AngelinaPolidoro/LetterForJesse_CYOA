About the Author

Angelina Polidoro is a student currently attending the University of Central Florida and pursuing a degree in Digital Media: Video Game Design. She begun Florida’s Bright Future’s college program at Florida SouthWestern State College at 14, earning her associates and graduating high school at 16. She then transferred to UCF with the goal of getting her master’s degree.

Her dedication and love of learning has made her appreciate all knowledge and work hard, but especially the arts. In and out of her college education, she has pursued her love of the arts, exploring a variety of mediums. She found a special interest in video game design when she began to watch YouTuber’s play games during the pandemic, fueling her childhood love of games and encouraging her career path.
