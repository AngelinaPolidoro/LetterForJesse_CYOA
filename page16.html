Letter for Jesse

Ending: A burnt letter.

You decide to destroy the letter and let Jesse go in the process. There was joy and happiness in your past, but everything changes, including your relationship. You think, no, hope that Jesse is living in fulfillment and happiness. That no matter what they do or the troubles they face, they will always survive and find enjoyment in all corners of life without you. As you grab a match and take the letter to your driveway, the sky is a muddled gray with hints of the orange sunset fading away. You know that dawn will come, and with it a newfound peace. You hope that Jesse has found the same peace. The lighter flickers and the paper slowly crackles and crumbles aways. You take a deep breath as the flame illuminates the final words of your letter before disappearing:

In the past I needed you. Now I cherish you. And in the future, I will always remember you.
